# 🔺 Shape Shout! — Free Printable Party Game Kit

**One peek. One voice. Two pads.** A blind-drawing showdown where one player memorizes a secret picture, then draws it from memory *while shouting instructions* — and real-world words are strictly illegal. Say the shape, not the thing!

**▶ Play it now: [devkushgowda.github.io/shape-shout-party-kit](https://devkushgowda.github.io/shape-shout-party-kit/)**

Perfect for families, classrooms, youth groups, office team events, and game nights. Ages 8 → 108. No sign-up, no ads, nothing to buy — just print and play.

## 🎲 How it works

1. **Pair up & wall off** — each team is a *Guide* and an *Artist*, back-to-back. No peeking, ever.
2. **The lightning peek** — the Guide studies a secret shape card for a few seconds, then it goes face-down. Your table picks the peek time (classic: 10 s).
3. **The timed chaos** — the Guide draws it from memory *while shouting geometric instructions*; the Artist draws only what they hear. You set the timer (classic: 60 s) — or use the built-in party timer on the website.
4. **Reveal** — line up the card, the Guide's drawing, and the Artist's drawing. Prepare to be humbled.
5. **Score & swap** — memory + precision + vibe vote − penalties. Every real-world word ("house!", "snowman!") costs a point.

Timing is a **house rule**: write your chosen peek and draw times on the poster, or use the **⏱ party timer** built into the website — set any times you like and it runs the peek phase and draw phase back-to-back with beeps.

## 🖥 On-screen party tools

The website is a full party console — no printing required to start playing:

- **Flash rounds** — pick a difficulty (Level 1–3 or 🎲 Surprise me) and the screen deals a random secret card: flashed for the peek, hidden for the draw countdown, revealed at the end for scoring. Cards don't repeat until the whole level has been played.
- **No-flash easy mode** — tick *"keep the card on screen"* and the card stays visible for the whole round; the round is just timed. Great for the youngest players.
- **⏱ Timer or ⏲ Stopwatch** — run the classic countdown at your own pace, or flip to the stopwatch and *record* how long a team actually took. Times can be saved to a team.
- **🏆 Teams & leaderboard** — add teams and player names, log each round's score, and watch the live ranking (points first, best time as tiebreaker). Everything is stored on your device only — no accounts, no server — and survives a refresh. One click resets the party.
- **Export filled-in awards** — pick a team and any of the 10 awards, and print the certificate with the names, score and date already filled in (choose "Save as PDF" in the print dialog to export it).

## 🚀 Two ways to print

1. **Easiest:** grab the ready-made PDFs from the [`pdf/`](pdf/) folder and print at **100% scale** (Letter or A4 both work — don't "fit to page").
2. **Interactive:** open the [website](https://devkushgowda.github.io/shape-shout-party-kit/) (or `index.html` locally) and use the toolbar to print everything or any single section. Turn on **"Background graphics"** in the print dialog.

**Tips:** use cardstock for the shape cards so marker doesn't show through, and everything prints fine in black & white too.

## 🧰 You'll need
