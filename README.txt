SHAPE SHOUT! — Printable Party Kit
==================================

Two ways to print:

1) EASIEST: open the PDFs in the /pdf folder and print at 100% scale.
   - shape-shout-FULL-KIT.pdf ... all 18 pages
   - 1-posters.pdf .............. 3 wall posters
   - 2-scorecards.pdf ........... team scorecards (2 per page — print 1 per 2 teams)
   - 3-shape-cards.pdf .......... 18 shape cards in 3 difficulty levels
   - 4-open-book.pdf ............ the shared shape-name chart (print 2 per table)
   - 5-certificates.pdf ......... 10 award certificates

2) INTERACTIVE: open index.html in any browser and use the toolbar buttons
   to print any section. Enable "background graphics" in the print dialog.
   Or just visit: https://devkushgowda.github.io/shape-shout-party-kit/

Tips: cardstock for the shape cards so marker doesn't show through; Letter or
A4 both work at 100% scale (do not "fit to page" — it's already sized).
